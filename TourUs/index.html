What is TourUs?

TourUs is a crowdsourcing platform for tourguides and tourists. I've often found tourist attractions to be unfulfilling. They aren't indicitive of a destination's true culture. These tourist sites are not what the people of a certain land experience on a daily basis. Experience adventure and the joy of the immersive experience with TourUs.

Here are some F.A.Q.

Why can’t I register?
First up: Don’t worry, this is by design. 196-25 has become a very popular class and more students want to take the class than we can accommodate. Because of this, we’re trying a new model of registration this semester. Under this model, registration will open after the first lecture. Our goal is to make sure that interested and motivated CS and ECE students can always take the class and we believe this new registration model will help us with that.

Why are there two sections of CS196?
CS 196 is the placeholder name for 100 level CS honors sections: CS196-25 and CS196-73. As their names suggest, these are the honor sections for CS125 and CS173. This course is CS 196-25.

When is the first class?
The first CS 196-25 class is on Tuesday, 25 August. It will be in 1404 Siebel. See you there.

What are the prerequisites?
Nothing! You’ve made it this far down the FAQ, so you’ve clearly got what it takes. All we ask is that you stay interested and work hard.

Do I need to buy any textbooks?
Nope! Each project will require different knowledge, therefore we do not approach the course from the perspective of a single text. Class content will be available in lectures and lecture handouts. In addition, the course staff and the internet will always be available to provide knowledge beyond what is covered in class.

How does grading work?
There will be a rubric released soon!

Should I be excited?
Yes. Very. I actually can't see a reason why you wouldn't be excited. In fact, I'm disappointed that you even had to look at the answer to this question. Anyhow, let's stop fussing about it, and let's get #hype.
