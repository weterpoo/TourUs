Here's what you can do

While this is a college course based at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, there are a lot of resources at those looking in from the outside. This includes our YouTube which includes our microlectures, and more. That's not a bad place to start. You can check it out here.

But wait, there's more! This class extends far past the classroom. While the University may host resources to help students, you can always do projects on your own. Grab a few buddies, come up with an idea, and adventure onward!

There is way too much out there in the world to be curious about. Don't just sit there, get up and make a change! Don't be afraid to dream big, godspeed!
